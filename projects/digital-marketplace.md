## Overview
A multi-vendor marketplace focused on digital assets and freelance services. It centralizes onboarding, catalog management, and fulfillment so creators can monetize quickly while keeping ownership of their brands.

## Key Contributions
- Designed a modular Next.js architecture with isolated seller and buyer experiences powered by a shared design system.
- Implemented Supabase for authentication, subscriptions, and role-based permissions across seller teams.
- Integrated Stripe Connect for automated payouts, tax handling, and granular revenue reporting.
- Built workflow automation to route service requests, escrow payments, and collect post-delivery reviews.

## Impact
- Reduced seller onboarding time from days to under 30 minutes with guided checklists and data validation.
- Enabled recurring revenue streams through subscription bundles and limited-time launch campaigns.
- Delivered product analytics dashboards that surfaced top-converting listings and retention insights.

- Harden multi-currency support ahead of international launch.
